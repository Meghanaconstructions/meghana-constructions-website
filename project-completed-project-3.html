Completed Residential Project

Meghana Homes Block-I & II

Project Overview

A completed two-block apartment project near the ECIL corridor

Meghana Homes Block-I & II is a completed residential apartment project on Road No. 4, M.J. Colony, Moula-Ali. The project includes typical floors for both blocks, each showing two 1625 sft homes with lift access and family-friendly circulation.

The project includes detailed specifications covering RCC framed construction, lightweight bricks, vitrified flooring, teak and flush doors, glazed kitchen and toilet finishes, generator support, security cameras, and rain water harvesting.

Key Highlights

Project Highlights

  • Block-I and Block-II both feature two 1625 sft homes.
  • 100% Vaastu, pleasant elevation, and user-friendly floor plans.
  • CC camera security, DG back-up, and car parking.
  • Close to colleges, malls, hospitals, and the ECIL bus corridor.
  • Well-detailed project with lift, security, flooring, and finish details.
