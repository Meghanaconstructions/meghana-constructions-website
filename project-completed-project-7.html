Completed Residential Project

Meghana Hills

Project Overview

Compact completed homes with strong Jawahar Nagar connectivity

Meghana Hills is a completed residential apartment project near MS Hospital on Jawahar Nagar Main Road, Moula-Ali. The project offers two 1175 sft homes per typical floor, making this one of the more compact completed apartment options in the portfolio.

The project includes Vaastu-led planning, user-friendly floor plans, rain water harvesting, HMWS and SB water provision, CC camera security, and DG back-up.
