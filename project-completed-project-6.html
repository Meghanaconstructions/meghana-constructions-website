Completed Residential Project

Meghana Arcade

Project Overview

Completed apartment homes at the Jawahar Nagar and Life Spring Hospital junction

Meghana Arcade is a completed residential apartment project in Jawahar Nagar near Life Spring Hospital, Moula-Ali. The project offers two 1600 sft homes with separate flat planning and a convenient junction-based neighborhood setting.

Its provided highlights include 100% Vaastu, premium quality construction, rain water harvesting, HMWS and SB water provision, CC camera security, and DG back-up. The project also benefits from strong recognition within a cluster of nearby Meghana developments.

Project Highlights
