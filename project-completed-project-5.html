Project Overview

Completed Krishna Nagar apartments with a stronger lifestyle pitch

This completed Meghana Homes project on Street No. 4, Krishna Nagar, NFC Road, ECIL is presented as a luxury apartment offering. The project offers two 1500 sft homes per floor, with a more lifestyle-led cover page and a practical two-home plan page.
