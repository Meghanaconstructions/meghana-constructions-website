Meghana Satyam Homes Block-I

Project Overview

A premium 2000 sft home format in Sardar Patel Nagar

Meghana Satyam Homes Block-I is presented as an ongoing residential apartment offering in Sardar Patel Nagar, Moula-Ali. The project features a spacious 2000 sft plan and a premium contemporary elevation suited to family living.

Its material notes call out UPVC windows with safety grills, quality sanitary fittings, and the same premium construction language seen across Meghana Constructions projects. The project is also close to ECIL-linked daily conveniences and transit access.
